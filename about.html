I am a professional software architect and developer evangelist, working in the Consumer IoT department of Deutsche Telekom in my day job. I am an expert in building and leading Open Source developer communities.

Home automation is my passion since childhood and in 2010, I have founded the very popular open Home Automation Bus (openHAB) project and am the project lead of the Eclipse SmartHome project, which is part of the IoT efforts of the Eclipse Foundation.

You can meet me at international conferences, where I regularly speak about all topics around smart homes.
